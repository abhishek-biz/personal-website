In The Beginning (Hello, World!)

Hi! My name is Abhishek, I'm 21 and this is the beginning of this new venture that I'm working on which is well, blogging and/or newsletter? Before I begin I want to thank David Pierce who is the chief editor of theverge.com whose writings inspired me to write something like that myself and rogosmodern.life for inspiring me to create a website like that for myself. I love writing, I've been filling up journals consistently for around 2-3 years now and honestly this is something that I've always wanted to do, not just the writing but putting myself out there! That said this is not a 'dear diary' type thing; My plan with this website is to provide at least some sort of value to the reader, maybe they learn something new or just enjoy the read but nevertheless I wont be posting slop. Although I highly doubt there will be any audience for this especially in India but I'm fine just doing it for myself too, to learn and most of all improve my writing. Honestly even if I get one or two concurrent readers I'm more than happy. Now, I'll be doing this weekly every Sunday and the main USP for this is going to be "The Things" (I need to think of a better name) where I will list down all the things that I have learned in the past week in short, easily digestible bullet points. One important thing, 0% AI will be used in ANY of my writings. I want to develop my own personal style. I'll be honest I am overthinking what to put on here considering if somebody discovers this website they'll surely be curious about the first post right. I would be. The things: This week I was really getting into 'To Kill a Mockingbird' by Harper Lee, I'm a bit past the middle here but don't expect a book review anytime soon because I read just 20 minutes barely (for now). "Sometimes I go about in pity for myself and all the while a great wind carries me across the sky" So this is a quote from one of the best TV shows ever written 'The Sopranos' I'm plan to finish it in a few weeks, I have to say though, I am REALLY enjoying it. I usually watch an episode and read Ron's writeup about it on sopranosautopsy.com I've discovered a ton of amazing stuff there you can check it out if you haven't. I've been listening to a ton of indie hindi music this past week, particularly I finished all of The Local Train's music which to be fair is just 2 albums but plan to get deeper into this rabbithole I'm really enjoying it, particularly because I don't listen to hindi songs much AT ALL. I never have. I was listening to Joe Rogan's podcasts with Duncan Trussell a lot this week while working out, someone recommended it to me one time. I haven't really thought much about it though honestly, I'm yet to form an opinion on that. So that's it for this week's "things"!! The list seems short, I thought I'd have more to say... 'It ain't much but it's honest work.' I'm still super stoked to publish this, work on the website technicality wise, write the material get some creativity flowing. I have a lot of plans for this, make a youtube channel maybe in the future, might as well engage the speaking skills and with neuroplasticity on my side, I can be the best writer and the best communicator and the best blah... positivity! I can add a music to this which I really enjoyed that week, some youtube video that blew my mind. Hell, I even create my portfolio on this thing, and who knows sometimes maybe even some parts of my personal life... There are just so many possibilities and I'm so willing to take on this journey!
